Search the ESMA MiCA register

The DEA MiCAR Tracker presents the EU's Markets in Crypto-Assets (MiCA / MiCAR) registers in one searchable place, based on public data from the ESMA interim MiCA register. Browse MiCA-authorised Crypto-Asset Service Providers (CASPs), e-money token (EMT) issuers, and entities flagged as non-compliant by country, competent authority, services and token. Updated weekly; download any register as CSV or JSON.
